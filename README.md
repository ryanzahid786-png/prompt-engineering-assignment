# Prompt Engineering Assignment

## Objective

This project compares Zero-shot Prompting and Few-shot Prompting for classifying customer support messages into three categories:

- Complaint
- Question
- Praise

The goal is to compare the performance of different AI models using both prompting techniques.

---

## Customer Support Messages

1. My order arrived two days late and the package was damaged.
2. How can I reset my account password?
3. Thank you for the quick response. Your support team was amazing!
4. I was charged twice for the same purchase. Please fix this.
5. Do you offer international shipping?
6. I really appreciate how friendly your customer service representatives are.
7. The product I received is not the one I ordered.
8. What is your return policy?
9. Your website is easy to use. Great job!
10. I've been waiting for a refund for over three weeks.

---

## Zero-shot Prompt

The AI was asked to classify the messages without providing any examples.

---

## Few-shot Prompt

The AI was asked to classify the same messages after being given three labeled examples.

---

## Models Used

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates the difference between Zero-shot and Few-shot prompting. Few-shot prompting generally provides more consistent results because the AI is guided with labeled examples before performing the task.
